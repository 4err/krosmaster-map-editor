To resize windows

Each window (Toolbox and help) is resizeable, simply grab an edge and pull.

To create and to move tokens

Holding left click on a token'll make it follow your mouse.

To delete a token

Right clicking on a token outside of the toolbox will delete it.

One 'Double Click' to control them all

On tokens and diagrams : it will alter their ring's colour. On decors and tiles : it will make them rotate.

How to snap ?

pieces in the Diagrams tab can snap together to align them easier. They gain that snapping ability if you release and regrab them after creation. Decors and Tiles snap with each other too.

To save your work as .png

Clicking on Create Picture will open a window. Chose a size in pixels, and a name for your picture then validate. Checking Symmetry will mirror tiles and decors toward center.
